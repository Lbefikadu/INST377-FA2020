This is the University of Maryland President, Darryll J. Pines

About Pines

UMD's first African American President

  1. Fun Facts Part 1

    Early Life

    • Darryll Pines was born in August 28th, 1964
    • He was born raised in Oakland, California
    • His parents had blue collar jobs.

  2. Fun Facts Part 2

    Growing Up

    • He recieved a scholarship to attend University of California-Berkley.
    • After attending Berkley, he continued onto MIT for a PhD in Aerospace Engineering
    • This degree was paired with a masters.

  3. Fun Facts Part 3

    Pines Today

    • Pines is currently the President of UMD.
    • Prior to this, he was a professor and a Dean for the Engineering school
    • He has been with the University for 25 years.
